About Pacific Trails Resort

Our Mission

Pacific Trails' mission is to bring the best experience possible so that all it's guests can enjoy the most out of their trip! It was founded with the aim of showcasing nature and allowing those that visit us a special window to the natural world without sacrificing any of the comforts that would make for a pleasant stay.

Environmental Promise

Pacific Trails Resort was founded with cooperation with the state's nature conservation programs to ensure that the natural environment would be preserved even in light of human presence and activity. The Resort is dedicated to donating a portion of its proceeds back towards environmental organization as well as the park's own conservation team!

Visit Us!

The many activities that the resort offers will cater to most nature-loving guests! From kayaking, hiking, bird watching, guided tours and more there are options for everyone, not including the resort's proximity to the beaches and shores of the golden coast.
